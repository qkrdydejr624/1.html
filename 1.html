Surface and paint damage repair

Transport, assembly, dynamic loads, dust particles, unfavourable weather conditions and any other negative environmental impact may cause damage to the surface coating of the rotor blades (paint cracks, paint flaking, etc.).

These instructions describe how to carry out paint repairs. If damage is detected in the leading edge (erosion) area of the rotor blade, proceed as described in instructions TD-esc-07-ger-xxx-10-004.

Be sure to read and understand all safety instructions before commencing work.

The photos/figures in this document serve as illustrations and may differ from the actual components.
